Taking Over The World:
Start with Mexico
Manage to convince all the drug cartels to serve you
Take this new army into Central and South America, where you will conquer all and rule with an iron fist
Manage to create enough stability in your new lands to create a second, more powerful arm, and take over, in order: Africa, Australia, and Asia
Easily conquer the rest of the world, as you already have over 50% of it
Build an fortress at the South Pole to serve as your palace
Become wizened by age, realize that the world function much better as a Democracy than as a Dictatorship, and at end of your lifetime create a central Terrestrial government that will, for the second time in history, unite the entire world under one flag. This time, they will do it will the full support of the people.
